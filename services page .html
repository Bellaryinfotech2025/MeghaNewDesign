Our Expertise and Specialities

The Al Naboodah Construction Group (ANCG) started operations in the UAE in the 1960s and today employs in excess of 14,000 people. ANCG is managed with a wealth of international expertise and specialises in the following areas of construction: civil engineering, building, MEP, ready-mix concrete and asphalt, and the supply of specialist plant and equipment.
