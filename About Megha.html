A Legacy of Excellence

ACTS' Journey in Construction Quality & Safety

Since ACTS' inception in 1996, we have established ourselves as the regional leader in construction consultancy services. With a deep focus on materials testing and geo-engineering, ACTS has earned a reputation for delivering comprehensive and reliable solutions throughout the Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Driven by a commitment to continuous improvement, ACTS has launched a journey of strategic expansion. Today, the company boasts six specialized divisions, each working seamlessly to provide clients with unparalleled expertise:

  • Testing Services: Rigorous material and environmental testing ensures project integrity and performance.
  • Inspection Services: Advanced technology and experienced inspectors guarantee quality and safety.
  • Certifications: Providing peace of mind through reliable certification programs.
  • Engineering Services: Comprehensive solutions for complex construction challenges.
  • Technical Standards & Solutions: Home to the world’s largest library of construction standards.
  • Training and Conferences: Industry leadership through extensive training and the largest concrete conference in the MEA region.
